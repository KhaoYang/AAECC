Our founding

Asian American Education and Culture Center (AAECC), headquartered in Chicago, USA, is registered in Chicago, Illinois, USA. Dealing with Eastern and Western cultures, they spread new educational concepts; cooperate and participate in community activities in the United States, and actively promote non-profit, non-political and non-religious organizations with the purpose of promoting education and cultural exchanges. The Yamei Center was launched in 2018 by several enthusiastic public welfare people. In 2019, it was registered in the federal government and officially became a non-profit organization for 501(C) 3 tax relief by the federal government and the Illinois state government in 2022.
