College Questions

The key components of the college application are your transcript, score reports, letters of recommendation, and application essay. Colleges will also ask you to list your extracurricular activities. Learn everything you need to know about college application .

In general, most students apply to 7-10 colleges. This is a good number to aim for, assuming that the applications you submit represent a broad variety of colleges. Usually, you will want to apply to 2-3 safety schools, 3-4 target schools, and 2-3 reach schools.

The lowest word counts for college essays are usually around 200 words (less than half a single-spaced page). You will rarely see a word limit higher than around 600 words (over one single-spaced page). College essays are usually pretty short: between 200 and 650 words.
