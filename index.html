AAECC offer a variety of coaching services to help you and your loved ones to move past the obstacles in your college application. Different Students Require Different Approaches to Apply

Feeling stuck? - Hello from AAECC. If you feel like you're at a crossroads in your college application, you are at RIGHT place. Even the strongest among us can feel lost, unsure, ambivalent, or unhappy at times. While providing a success consultation service, AAECC can help you understand and manage each and every obstacle during your college application. We know how to do it successfully. Individualized, Customized ​​Plans

Your path to college begins in your first year in high school as you make yourself college ready. Grades and test scores are important factors in college admissions, but admissions officers are also looking for curious and engaged candidates who will round out a diverse first-year class. The AAECC can help you to prepare yourself for excellence as early as your freshman year. Come and talk to us!
